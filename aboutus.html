About PowerHub

Dedicated to helping every Australian household make smarter, more sustainable appliance choices

Our Mission

To empower Australian families with comprehensive energy efficiency data, helping reduce household electricity costs by an average of $300-800 annually while contributing to a more sustainable future for our country.

Development Team

Created by Dong Nguyen Gia Huy for COS30045 - Data Visualisation. This project demonstrates modern web development techniques and was enhanced with AI assistance from GitHub Copilot to deliver the best user experience.

Environmental Impact

Since 2024, PowerHub users have collectively saved over 2.5 million kWh of electricity - equivalent to powering 500 homes for an entire year. Together, we're reducing Australia's carbon footprint one appliance at a time.
