Television Energy Consumption in Australia

A Data-Driven Analysis of Energy Efficiency Trends

As Australian households increasingly prioritise energy efficiency and sustainability, understanding the power consumption patterns of televisions becomes crucial for informed consumer decisions. This data story explores energy consumption trends across different television technologies, brands, and screen sizes using comprehensive market data.

Key Research Questions

  • Which screen technologies dominate the Australian television market?
  • How do different brands and models compare in terms of market presence?
  • What is the relationship between screen size and power consumption?

Data Story: Three Key Insights

Through comprehensive analysis of television market data, three clear patterns emerge that inform energy-conscious purchasing decisions.

1. Screen Technology Market Share

The Australian television market shows a clear preference for LED technology, which offers an optimal balance between energy efficiency and affordability. Understanding the distribution of screen technologies helps consumers identify the most common and well-supported options in the market.

2. Brand Market Presence

Market diversity in television brands reflects consumer choice and competitive pricing. This analysis reveals which manufacturers offer the widest range of models, potentially indicating market leadership and research investment in energy-efficient technologies.

Figure 2: Number of television models per brand in the Australian market. The distribution indicates market competition levels and brand investment in product diversity.

3. Screen Size and Energy Consumption Relationship

The relationship between screen size and power consumption is fundamental for energy-conscious consumers. This analysis reveals how television size directly impacts household electricity usage, enabling informed decisions about the environmental and financial implications of screen size choices.

About the Data

Data Source and Collection

The television energy consumption dataset was compiled from publicly available product specifications and energy rating databases. Data includes screen technologies, brand information, model specifications, screen sizes, and power consumption measurements across a representative sample of televisions available in the Australian market.

Data Quality and Limitations

The dataset represents a snapshot of the television market and may not capture all available models or recent releases. Power consumption figures are based on manufacturer specifications under standard testing conditions and may vary with actual usage patterns.

Ethics and Privacy Considerations

All data utilised in this analysis consists of publicly available product information. No personal consumer data was collected or analysed. The research adheres to ethical guidelines for data usage and presentation, ensuring accuracy and responsible interpretation of findings.
