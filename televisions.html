Smart TV Energy Guide

Find the perfect balance between stunning picture quality and energy efficiency for your Australian home

LED Televisions

Modern LED displays offer exceptional energy efficiency with vibrant colors and sharp detail. Average power consumption: 80-150W. Perfect for everyday viewing with up to 60% less energy usage than older models.

OLED Premium TVs

Experience cinema-quality visuals with OLED technology that delivers perfect blacks and infinite contrast. Smart power management automatically adjusts brightness, consuming only 120-200W while providing superior picture quality.

Smart Energy Features

Intelligent auto-brightness, eco-mode settings, and sleep timers reduce power consumption by up to 30%. Motion sensors detect when you're away and automatically switch to power-saving mode.
